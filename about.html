About Us

Aura Dance Academy

Welcome to Aura Dance Academy Located in the heart of Udupi, Aura Dance Academy has been a cornerstone of the local dance community since 2019. Nestled in a vibrant neighborhood, our academy offers a welcoming and inspiring environment where dancers of all ages and skill levels can thrive.

Our Mission

At our academy, our mission is to provide exceptional dance education while fostering a supportive and inclusive community.. Through our commitment to excellence and dedication to each individual's journey, we aim to inspire a lifelong love of dance in every dancer who walks through our doors.
